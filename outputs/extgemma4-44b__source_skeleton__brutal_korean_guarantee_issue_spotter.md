### 1. Applicable Legal Framework
The analysis assumes the jurisdiction of the **Republic of Korea** and the application of the **Civil Act (민법)** and the **Civil Procedure Act (민사소송법)**. Key concepts include:
*   **Joint and Several Liability (연대채무):** When multiple parties are obligated to perform the same obligation, and each can be held for the full amount.
*   **Assignment of Claims (채권양도):** The transfer of a creditor's right to a third party, which must be properly notified to the debtor.
*   **Inheritance Renunciation (상속포기) and Limited Acceptance (한정승인):** Options for heirs to decline or limit their liability for the deceased's debts, subject to strict deadlines.
*   **Acceleration of Debt (기한의 이익 상실):** A clause that allows a creditor to demand full payment before the original due date if certain conditions (e.g., default) are met.
